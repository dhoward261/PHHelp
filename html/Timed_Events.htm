Timed Events
Timed Events are simply actions that are solely based on the system clock.  Timed Events are declared in the Timed Events section of the PowerHome Explorer.  Timed Events can also be created from within macros and formulas.  When a timed event is executed, its action is either a Macro, Send Keys, Raw Formula, or a Device Control string.  Timed Events are based upon a timestamp (specific time and date) and can have either a recurring frequency (executes and automatically creates a copy of itself a specified interval in the future) or be a one shot (executes one time and then is destroyed).
