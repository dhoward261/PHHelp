FAQ: UNDO/REDO
Knowledge Notes

A CTRL-Z will UNDO changes as most Windows programs do. Within the PowerHome Explorer, an F10 will UNDO everything done on the current detail window. Once you leave a detail window, the changes are permanent. A CTRL-Y will REDO actions, as with most Windows programs.

When you delete a macro line, two things happen...

  • First, the line is deleted, then
  • the rest of the lines are renumbered.
So the first CTRL-Z undoes the renumbering and the second CTRL-Z undoes the delete.

F10 will “Undo All” (up to the last save).

There is also a CTRL-Y for redo.

Thus if you're working on a screen and have made multiple changes, a single F10 will revert you back to where you were when you first entered the screen.

You can also ctrl-z your way back to this point. But if you hit F5 (Save/Refresh) or navigate to a different Explorer detail screen, your changes are saved to the database and are permanent.

These 3 options are also available and shown on the PH Explorer toolbar (last 3 buttons) and the "Edit" menu.
