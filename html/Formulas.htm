Formulas
Formulas are where most of your automation power is derived.  Formulas are used throughout PowerHome and are an essential component of macros, triggers, and PSP (PowerHome Server Pages).  Formulas can return text, numeric, and date/time values.  Before a formula is evaluated, variable substitution is performed upon it.  Once variable substitution is complete, the formula is passed to the formula evaluator.  The results of the formula are then passed back.  Formulas themselves can contain a number of functions.  The available Formula Functions are documented within the Formulas section.
